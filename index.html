Signed Network Dataset Repository

Overview

Welcome to the Signed Network Dataset Repository (SigNET Repo), your gateway to a diverse collection of signed network datasets. Our repository is a valuable resource for researchers, data enthusiasts, and anyone interested in exploring the intricate dynamics of signed networks. In the world of signed networks, relationships between entities can be both positive and negative, reflecting the complexities of human interactions. Our repository offers datasets from various domains, including social networks, trust networks and more. Whether you're studying commmunity detection, link and sign prediction, trust prediction, conflict resolution, node classification, or link strength prediction, these datasets provide the foundation for innovative research and insights. Join us on a journey through the fascinating world of signed networks and contribute to the advancement of network science and social analysis.
